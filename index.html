About GroundZero

Every believer is God’s masterpiece, an expression of His genius and His work of art, created by design to fulfill a divine purpose. God has equipped every believer with spiritual gifts, abilities, experiences, and temperament for the purpose of serving and building the Local Church. Discovery and training programs are run in order to place the right people, for the right tasks.

God loves people. Both as leaders and lay people we strive to communicate the uncompromised Gospel in a way that ordinary people can understand and respond. Various specialized services are held throughout the year in order to present the Good News in a logical and truthful manner.

God’s design is that every believer should not remain spiritual infants, but grow to maturity, taking responsibility for themselves and supporting those around them.
